There are three operator
1. unary operator
    - it will required only one operand
2. binary operator
    - it will required two operand
3. ternary operator
    - it will required three operand


Binary Operator:
1. Airthmatic Operator : + , - , * , / , % , **
2. Assignment Operator : = , (+=, -=, *=, /=, %=, **=) - short hand property
3. Relational Operator / comparision operator : == , != , < , > , <= , >=
4. Logical Operator : && , || , !
5. Bitwise Operator : & , | , ^
